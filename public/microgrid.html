MICROGRID ENERGY MANAGER

You run a microgrid for 24 hours. Your job: minimize your electricity bill without blacking out.

You have:

  • 5 kW solar panels — free energy, but only when the sun shines
  • 10 kWh battery — store cheap energy, use it when prices spike
  • Grid connection — buy or sell power, but prices change every hour
  • House loads — 1-6 kW demand you can't control

Rules:

  • Grid price varies: night €0.08, day €0.15, peak €0.25/kWh
  • Drawing >5 kW from grid = €0.50/kW penalty per 15-min block
  • Blackout (unmet load) = €2 penalty per 15-min block
  • Selling to grid earns 80% of current price
  • Battery: max 5 kW charge/discharge, 90% round-trip efficiency
